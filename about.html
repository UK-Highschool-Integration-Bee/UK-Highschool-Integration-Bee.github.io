About the UK High School Integration Bee

The UK High School Integration Bee is a national mathematics competition designed to challenge students to apply A-Level integration in creative and logical ways. Students from schools across the UK participate, solving challenging problems under timed conditions, with the opportunity to qualify for a final in-person round.

Recommended for Year 12–13 students, the competition is open to all UK school students. Schools register their students as a group, but single-student entries are allowed if supervised by a teacher or invigilator.

Our goal is to make integration fun, exciting, and intellectually stimulating, while encouraging students to explore the beauty of mathematics. With playful math visuals and a supportive competition environment, participants can enjoy a memorable and rewarding experience.
