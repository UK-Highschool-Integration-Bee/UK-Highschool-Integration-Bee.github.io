Register

Schools register as a group, submitting a single registration form for all participating students. Individual students may also participate if they provide an invigilator.

Registration closes on 6 March. We recommend early registration to ensure your school receives all competition materials on time.

Participation is calculator-free. Students should bring pens, paper, and a clear workspace for the Round 1 paper.
