Overview

The UK High School Integration Bee is a national mathematics competition designed to challenge students in Year 12–13 (and other interested students) to think creatively and logically using A-level integration.

Round 1 is hosted in schools across the UK as a one-hour multiple-choice paper. The top-performing students qualify for the national final, where participants compete in a round-robin format and then knockout rounds.

This competition encourages problem-solving, logical thinking, and mathematical insight. It’s a great opportunity to challenge yourself and compete with students from across the country.

Participation in the Integration Bee is not only fun but also looks excellent on university applications and helps develop important problem-solving skills.
