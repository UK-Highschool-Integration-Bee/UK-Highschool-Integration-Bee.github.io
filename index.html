Overview

The UK High School Integration Bee is a national mathematics competition designed to challenge students using A-level integration in creative, logical, and non-routine ways.

The competition is recommended for Year 12–13 students, but is open to all secondary school students. Schools across the UK host the first round, with top performers qualifying for a national final.

All problems are multiple choice. No calculators or technology are permitted. The focus is on clarity of thought, problem-solving, and mathematical insight.
