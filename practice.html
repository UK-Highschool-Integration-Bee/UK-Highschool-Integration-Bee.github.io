Practice

To prepare for the Integration Bee, we recommend students practice A-level integration questions and familiarise themselves with multiple-choice problem solving.

We will provide a sample practice PDF for schools to download and distribute. The practice PDF will mirror the types of questions one might expect from a round 1 paper, with questions getting progressively harder.

Practicing on a whiteboard or with scratch paper is encouraged, as calculators are not allowed in the competition.
